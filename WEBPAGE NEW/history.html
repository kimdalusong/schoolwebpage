CRT HISTORY

CRT Overview

1998

CRT Gapan was transferred to its new site in Sto. Nino, Gapan City.

1999

Bestowed Government Permit from CHED to operate BS in Information Technology, one of the first Colleges in Nueva Ecija to offer such course.

2000

CRT opened its extension campus at Our Lady of Sacred Heart Academy (OLSHA) in Guimba, Nueva Ecija. College for Research & Technology was one of the framers of Competency Based Training under the Philippine – Australia Quality Technical and Vocational Education and Training (PAQTVET)

2001

Awarded accreditation from TESDA – RO3 to be an Accredited Assessment Center for Data Encoder.

Received Government Recognition from TESDA to operate programs: Three – Year Computer Engineering and Hardware Technology (NTR – 03204); Two – Year Electronics and Computer Technician (NTR – 03205); Two – Year Computer Secretarial (NTR – 03202); and Two – Year Computer Science in Information Technology (Computer Programming WTR 03140)

estowed Government Recognition from CHED of BS in Information Technology (HER 035)

2002

Awarded Government Permit from CHED to operate BS Business Administration (Major in Entrepreneurial Management HET 037, s. 2002) Engr. Reynato C. Arimbuyutan (College President) became the founding president of Nueva Ecija Association of Technical – Vocational Institutions (NEATVI)

2003

Construction of the new CRT – Guimba branch began. The location is along Parairo St., Saranay Dist., Guimba, Nueva Ecija.

2004

Transfer of CRT – Guimba to its new location in Parairo St., Saranay Dist., Guimba, Nueva Ecija Engr.

Reynato C. Arimbuyutan became the founding Vice President of the Philippine Society of Information Technology Educators (PSITE) – Region 3.

Bestowed Government Recognition from TESDA to CRT Cabanatuan to operate Two – Year Associate in Hotel & Restaurant Management NC Level II (WTR 0403042012)
